Koan 6: Nothing Goes Away
For Koan 6, I have chosen to give an explanation of the Internet Archive, functioning as a blog post.
Koan 6 focuses on how nothing ever truly goes away. The Internet Archive is a good example of this, more specifically the WayBack Machine. The goal of the website is to archive as much data as possible.
In addition to the Internet Archive, the WayBack Machine's goal is to archive every website at every point of its lifespan. This website is a good example of how nothing truly goes away.
